## Layout Strategy

### Section 1: Hero
**Purpose**: Immediately resonate with the fragmentation pain point and present ProductFlow as the solution.

**Layout**:
- Left column (60%): Headline, subheadline, dual CTAs, trust indicator
- Right column (40%): Product screenshot or animated UI preview

**Content Strategy**:
- **Headline**: "Stop Managing Your Product Across 5 Different Tools"
- **Subheadline**: "ProductFlow consolidates your roadmap planning, user feedback, and sprint execution into one platform. Get your entire product team alignedâ€”finally."
- **Primary CTA**: "Start Free Trial" (Green button, links to https://app.productflow.io/signup)
- **Secondary CTA**: "Schedule Demo" (Outlined button, opens calendar widget)
- **Trust Indicator**: "No credit card required â€¢ 14-day free trial â€¢ SOC 2 Type II certified"

**Rationale**: Problem-focused headline immediately validates the visitor's frustration. Dual CTAs accommodate different buying stages (self-serve vs. guided). Trust indicators reduce friction upfront.
