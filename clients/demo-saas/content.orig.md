### Question 1
**How long does implementation take?**

Most teams are up and running in under 5 minutes. Simply connect your existing tools, import your data automatically, and start building your roadmap. We also offer free onboarding sessions if you need guidance.

### Question 2
**Do I need technical resources to set up integrations?**

No developer required. All 50+ integrations connect with one click through OAuth. Your data syncs automatically in real-time.

### Question 3
**What happens to our data if we cancel?**

You own your data, always. Export everything in standard formats (CSV, JSON) at any time. We'll retain your data for 30 days after cancellation, then permanently delete it per your request.

### Question 4
**Is ProductFlow secure enough for enterprise use?**

Yes. We're SOC 2 Type II certified, GDPR compliant, and use bank-level 256-bit encryption. Enterprise plans include SSO/SAML, advanced security controls, and custom data retention policies.

### Question 5

Absolutely. We provide free data migration assistance for all paid plans. Our team will help you import roadmaps, feedback, and historical data from spreadsheets, Trello, Jira, or any other tool.

### Question 6

You can upgrade anytime with prorated billing. We'll only charge for the difference based on your remaining billing period.
