## Frequently Asked Questions

**What if I don't like the coffee?**

Your first bag comes with our satisfaction guarantee. If it's not for you, we'll replace it free ‚Äî no questions asked. We want you to love what you're drinking, and we're confident enough in our coffee to back that up.

---

**Can I pause or cancel my subscription?**

Anytime. No contracts, no cancellation fees, no jumping through hoops. Manage everything from your account in two clicks. Going on vacation? Pause it. Want to skip a month? Done. Need to cancel? We'll miss you, but there's no penalty.

---

**How fresh is the coffee when it arrives?**

We roast every week and ship within days. Every bag has the roast date printed on the label so you know exactly what you're getting. Most grocery store coffee was roasted weeks or even months ago. Yours? Less than a week old when it hits your doorstep.

---

**Where do you ship?**

Everywhere in the USA. All subscriptions include free shipping, whether you're in Portland or Portland (Maine). We pack your beans in heat-sealed bags with one-way valves to lock in freshness during transit.

---

**Can I choose my coffee or is it a surprise?**

Both! Explorer subscribers get our roaster's choice ‚Äî a great way to discover new favorites and trust our expertise. Enthusiast and Obsessed subscribers can choose their preferred origins each month. Want all Ethiopia? You got it. Prefer variety? Mix and match to your heart's content.

**How does billing work?**

You're billed monthly on the same date you signed up. You can change your plan, skip a month, or cancel anytime before your next billing date. No surprise charges. No auto-renewals you forgot about. Just straightforward billing that puts you in control.

**What's the difference between the plans?**

All plans get the same exceptional quality and freshness. Explorer gives you one bag of our choice ‚Äî perfect for individuals or anyone who wants to be surprised. **What if I need more (or less) coffee?**

Change your plan anytime. Need an extra bag this month because guests are coming? Add it. Want to downsize for a while? Switch tiers. Your subscription works for your life, not the other way around.

## Design Notes for Developer

**Color Implementation:**
- Headlines: Deep burgundy (#8B2635)
- Primary CTAs: Deep burgundy background with cream text
- Secondary accents/hover states: Burnt orange (#CC5500)
- Background: Cream/off-white (#FAF7F2)
- Body text: Deep burgundy (#8B2635)

**Typography Recommendations:**
- Headlines: A warm serif with character (consider: Freight, Lora, or Merriweather)
- Body copy: Clean, readable sans-serif (consider: Inter, Source Sans, or Open Sans)
- Keep generous line height (1.6-1.8) for readability

**CTA Buttons:**
- Default: Deep burgundy background, cream text, subtle shadow
- Hover: Burnt orange background, cream text
- Make them large and finger-friendly (minimum 44px height)

**Imagery Style:**
- Warm, natural lighting
- Real kitchen environments (not sterile studios)
- Show hands interacting with coffee
- Include steam, texture, morning light
- Avoid overly staged product shots

**Mobile Considerations:**
- Stack pricing cards vertically on mobile
- Make FAQ accordion touch-friendly
- Ensure CTAs remain visible and accessible
- Consider sticky "Subscribe" button on scroll
